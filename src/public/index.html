A Walk In My Shoes!

On June 17, 2003 I became a mother for the first time. From the first time I laid eyes on my little bundle, I knew I was in love. For almost 14 years I have made sacrifices to stay home and raise my children. Some days I question my decision. But then my day changes and I couldnt imagine anything different. They make me laugh. They make me cry. They make me love life!

In My Spare Time

In my spare time I take care of our little farm, milking the goat, feeding baby goats, gathering eggs from our chickens, and tending to our fruit and vegetable garden. I love to spin yarn from the wool I harvest from my two sheep. When I have some quiet time I also like to quilt. I am a third degree black belt in Tae Kwon Do and I teach martial arts in my non profit Christian-based martial arts school in Stevensville. I love to sing. Music has always been a big part of my life and I sing in church often. I also play the roll of school teacher to my two boys. I have been homeschooling them since my oldest son, Jonathan entered Kindergarden, 10 years ago. 4-H is a big part of our lives as well. I am the Organizational leader in our 4-H club and am the head chairman to the Ravalli County Small Animal Committee. Summer is my favorite time of year and I spend most of my time outside whenever possible. I love being out in the country, enjoying the quiet, and relaxing under the big, blue Montana sky.
